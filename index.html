About PlotSure Connect

Our Mission

To combat land fraud and improve trust in land transactions in Bugesera, Rwanda, by providing a transparent, secure platform that showcases verified land listings with complete documentation.

The Problem We Solve

According to the Rwanda Land Management Authority (2022), Bugesera is one of the districts experiencing a rise in land disputes and ownership conflicts. Land fraud and incomplete ownership records are persistent issues within Rwanda's real estate market. Many buyers fall victim to scams, false land claims, and inaccurate or outdated documentation.

Our Solution

PlotSure Connect is a digital platform where verified commission agents post authenticated land listings on behalf of landowners. Every listing includes:

  • ‚úÖ Scanned official land ownership documents (plot titles/certificates)
  • ‚úÖ High-quality photos and videos of the actual plot
  • ‚úÖ Verified landowner information and permissions
  • ‚úÖ Transparent pricing and plot specifications
  • ‚úÖ Direct communication with trusted brokers

Built for Trust

We understand the challenges faced by buyers, especially those in the diaspora, when purchasing land remotely. Our platform ensures complete transparency, reducing fraud-related disputes and building confidence in land transactions throughout Bugesera.
